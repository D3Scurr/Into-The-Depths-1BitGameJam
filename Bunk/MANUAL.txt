HOW TO PLAY

HOW TO WIN?

You can't.
Dodge the obstacles, bunk into objects and get as high of a score as you can.

Controls:
arrow keys - move player
space - go into bunk state
p - in game pause

BUNK STATE
You can activate bunk state by having >1 of bunk points (right bottom of the panel) that you normally get by filling up
the bunk meter (right top of the panel) it charges over time and by bunking horizontally into an object.

In bunk state you are invincible and gain points from bunking into objects falling at you.
Also if you bunk horizontally you gain a bunk point.

Options
You can change the scale of the window by entering a value in Scale.txt (the default is 3)

HAVE FUN PLAYING THE GAME!!!
